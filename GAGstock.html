Seeds
Seeds that the player can plant in their garden to harvest as crops. One of the ways of acquisition is the Seed Shop.
Gears
Tools that the player can use to enhance their gardening through buffs. One of the ways of acquisition is the Gear Shop.
Seed Shop
A non-player character that offers a stock of seeds for purchase for ingame currency. This stock refreshes every 5 minutes.
Gear Shop
A non-player character that offers a stock of gears for purchase for ingame currency. This stock refreshes every 5 minutes.
